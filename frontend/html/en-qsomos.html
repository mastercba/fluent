About Us

LEADING ONLINE LANGUAGE LEARNING


FluentSpeaking is a product of LANGUACOM Group, leader in language teaching in a personalized and live in Europe and America. The company was founded in Paris in 1981 with the aim of offering students a way to learn languages ​​by phone (Learning by Phoning ®) and sparing anywhere traffic, parking, waiting times, etc..

Live classes and custom are the essence of success FluentSpeaking.
We are a very select team with over 30 years experience, primarily consisting of professional technical graduates very prestigious universities and institutes in Europe and America, with a passion for teaching languages.
Our team of Native American and European teachers are certified in language teaching and committed to your success.
Our excellent education along with our experience in the business of teaching languages​​, are important achievements in our customers.

FluentSpeaking caters to students across Europe and North America. The offices are located in Sttugart and Miami.
